About

Welcome to Zifan Zhang’s Website!

I am currently a second-year master’s student at the Ohio State University with a major in electrical and computer engineering. I am now working with Professor Jia(Kevin) Liu’s group and will be graduating this May. I received my bachelor degree at the Ohio State University with a major in electrical and computer engineering as well.

With a background in both my bachelor’s and master’s degrees in electrical and computer engineering from the Ohio State University, my research interests lie in multi-agent reinforcement learning, machine learning security, and computer networking. I am passionate about designing secure and robust multi-agent machine learning systems for computer network applications, and I am excited to contribute to the field.

I will join Professor Yuchen Liu‘s Group at North Carolina State University in 2023 Fall as a PhD student.

On this web page, you will find information about my research projects, publications, and academic achievements. Please feel free to explore and connect with me on my academic pursuits. Thank you for visiting my personal web page!
